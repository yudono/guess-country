How to Play

A secret country is chosen at random. Your job is to guess which one.

After each guess, you get two clues:

↑ Direction — an arrow pointing from your guess toward the target country.
H Temperature — how close you are. From "Freezing" (far) to "On Fire" (very close).

The proximity bar at the top fills up as you get warmer.

In Unlimited mode, guess as many times as you want. In Daily mode, you get 6 guesses — same country for everyone.
